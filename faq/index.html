RWBY Adventures Frequently Asked Questions




This section contains a wealth of information, related to RWBY Adventures and its commands. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us. If you want to report an issue, type r!report with your issue.

General questions

How can I report a bug?

You can report a bug/issue by doing r!report .
You can also include pictures :
- Drag and Drop the picture.
- Comment the picture : "r!report" and add your message.

Example :
r!report I think I lost a character.

We will reach you back.

What can you do with the bot?

You can earn characters by opening Loot Boxes , Trade with other people or Sell/Buy them from the market.
You can also do Dungeons , Arenas , Gambling , Duelling and much more.

How can you earn money (Liens)?

The best way to earn Lien is to complete Dungeons (Be sure to entirely visit it!) and selling on the market.
Gambling and the Daily Reward are good sources of Liens as well.

What is the difference between the different Loot Boxes?

Common: Easily accessible, can be acquired via drops or the shop for a cheap price.
Rare : Easily accessible, can rarely be acquired via drops or the shop for a higher price.
Limited : Can only be acquired via Events or Patreon pledges.
Special : Can only be acquired during Special Events, it will always loot a character with the said category.

Which is better, Value or Rarity?

This question is very subjective because it will depend on what you want to use the character for rather than try and choose one over the other for all situations.
Value will help you during duels as they will be stronger the higher the value;
Rarity is more for collection though it has significant influence on stats as well.
The aim is to have High Value as well as a High Rarity .
It is recommended for you to use r!comp to compare and predict what a character will become like with higher level.

How do Mission days work?

1 day is approximatly 24 messages which will always be rounded UP , meaning if there was only 1 message left to finish a mission, it will still show up as 1 day.

Why are the prices on the market so high?

The prices are set by players and everyone is free to set their own price but one thing to keep in mind while selling or buying a character is how valuable it is to eveyone else.
Limiteds will always have naturally high prices because they are simply rare and so will high value/rarity characters.
The economy is based on the offer and the demand, the more people have to offer : the cheaper it will become.
You can not insult anyone for putting a high price on a listing.

Why are Backpacks so expensive?

Originally, players were limited to having only 10 characters . In these times, the bot was small and the database non-existent.
Then the database changed and people were allowed to buy only one 10 slots backpack and have up to 25 slots.
Finally, we changed the server recently and you now have 30 slots by default.
A backpack is at a lower price , and has 20 slots .

How do I earn Loot Boxes?

You can have your Daily Loot Boxes, 3 per day (the cooldown is written if you do r!me ) by talking.
You can buy either Rare or Normal loot boxes in the shop ( r!shop ).
You can find Normal and Rare Loot Boxes in Dungeons .

What are OC Characters?

OC stands for Original Character . During an OC Contest, you can submit your own OC and some of them were chosen to join the RWBY crew.
Limited, Events and Patreon

Do I need to pledge to Patreon to get Limited Characters :(?

No.
Patreon is absoloutely not needed in order to get any Limited Character in the bot.
It's only there as Support for the owner and to get something nice in return.
All you need to do is simply show up for events and you will be able to get Limited Lootboxes and Characters .
You can also Trade and Buy those characters.

How can I earn Limited Loot Boxes?

Limited Loot Boxes are limited for a reason and thereore are harder to get than Common or Rare Loot Boxes .
You can get them by participating in events hosted by the bot such as Special Arenas (see next question), where you MUST show up to in order to get a Loot Box.
Or you can win a Tournament and get a certain amount of Loot Boxes.

What are the bot's events?

There are currently 4 kinds of Events (suggestions are welcome) :
There are Special Arenas , Seasonal Events , Tournaments and Random Events .
To always stay notified for an upcoming event make sure to use the r!signup command or follow our server's #events announcement channel.

Is there any specific Characters that are special in their way?

Villains : Salem's Inner Circle, White Fang, Cinder's Faction, Tock, Young Salem and Ozma are only lootable in Limited Loot Boxes during an arena.
Seasons Edition : Only lootable during their respective seasons, for example, Summer Editions are only lootable during the summer.
Team STQR : Only lootable during a Team STQR Event.
Zwei : Only lootable during a Zwei Event.

How can I know when there is a special arena?

The bot will send you a message 5 minutes before the arena starts if you did the command :
- r!signup
Gambling

What are the loots I can get?

❅ 3 Rare Loot Boxes on screen => 2 rare loot boxes for you.
❅ 2 Rare Loot Boxes on screen => 1 rare loot box for you.
❅ 3 Loot Boxes on screen => 2 loot boxes for you.
❅ 2 Loot Boxes on screen => 1 loot box for you.
❅ 3 Characters on screen => 1 random character for you.
❅ 3 Arms on screen => 1 arm for you.
❅ 3 XP on screen => A lot of XP for you.
❅ 2 XP on screen => Some XP for you.
❅ 3 Coins on screen => A lot of money for you.
❅ 2 Coins on screen => Some money for you.
